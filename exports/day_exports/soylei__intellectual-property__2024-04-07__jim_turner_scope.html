Application in google: <https://patents.google.com/patent/WO2021055815A1/en> It's an extremely broad family of mono di and triglycerides. Must contain two components with composition greater than or equal to 1 ppm of the 2nd component. This is a double patenting work-around. Since veg oils and their derivatives are already mixtures they naturally fit this requirement. What's being allowed is claims 1-15. Independent claim 1 is for a method of producing asphalt including the binder and SESO or SESO-related additives. The dependent claims introduce polymer and specifically define SESO. Claim 14 claims the product resulting from the Claim 1 method. Independent claim 15 is a product claim of the form of Claim 1. The rest of the claims are mostly product claim variants of the allowed method claims. SESO with RAP. SESO in shingles. ISURF is already pursuing these divisionals in the US
