Further details below. Baker and Andrew made the following samples all at 33% solids loading: • 10% SBR, 90% AESO • 10% SBR, 90% AEHOSO • 20% SBR, 80% AESO • 20% SBR, 80% AEHOSO These were poured into a silicone mold with the activator &amp; initiator and placed into a dehydrating oven. When I checked on them this morning, they had developed a clear polymer skin; however, the container was still mostly water.
